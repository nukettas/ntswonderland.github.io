How would you feel if you had seen him again?

Written By NT June 3, 2023
Romance

For Sophie, this was just another regular day. She woke up, ate breakfast, and headed to work. As she entered the coffee shop where she works, she said hello to her regular customers. She was aware of what this day would entail. makes coffee and begins to close the store three and a half hours later. The day at work went by quite gradually and steadily.

When she was steaming the oat milk she had a feeling that someone was staring at her. She had no idea that seven years later, she would run into him again. However, he was standing squarely in front of her, gazing into her eyes, and grinning a warm, friendly smile. While the client waited for her final cup of coffee for the day, she apologized for burning the milk. She was left with no choice but to stare into the question marks in his eyes as he continued to smile and wait fervently. He answered with a kind "hi" when she could finally utter it. It's been a while, she remarked. I was waiting for you, he replied.
