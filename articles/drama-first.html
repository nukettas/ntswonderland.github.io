What is your dream family?

Written By NT June 12, 2023
Drama

As she heard curses and yelling from downstairs, she tried to stay calm, resisting the urge to eavesdrop on their conversation. However, she found it impossible to ignore. She felt compelled to listen to every single word, ensuring that no one was getting hurt, while also keeping an ear out for approaching footsteps so she could pretend to be asleep.

After the first set of footsteps passed by her room, she held her breath, she anxiously waited for the second. Although she couldn't bring herself to close her eyes, she remained alert. As soon as the second set of footsteps faded away, she reached for her headphones and immersed herself in a dream of her ideal family.

In her dream, she had a peaceful, loving, and happy family. She imagined herself with four children who adored her and craved her attention. Her husband was caring, handsome, and highly respected, and their home was warm and secure, with sturdy doors. Together, they set the dinner table, with music playing softly in the background. They exchanged affectionate kisses on the cheek whenever they passed each other, creating an atmosphere of love and intimacy.

After a brief dance break, the table was finally ready. Just as they were about to sit down with their children, her husband gently took her hand, looked into her eyes, and reassured her, "I will never hurt you." He pressed her against his chest, expressing his unwavering love and promising to protect her from any harm. Overwhelmed by his words, she drifted off to sleep, her tears still moist on her cheeks.
