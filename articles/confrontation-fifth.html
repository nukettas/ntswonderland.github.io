Have you ever tried to maintain a relationship alone?

Written By NT June 29, 2023
Confrontation

As they sat in the living room playing Trivia with their friends, she felt a moment of detachment. It dawned on her that he hadn't contributed to any of the preparations – no help with cooking, cleaning, or even tidying the room. Despite asking him a couple of times, he always seemed preoccupied with phone calls, emails, or gaming. It wasn't a new behavior; she remembered instances before. "Lisa, Lisa, it's your turn!" the others called out, interrupting her thoughts. "Sorry," she smiled, "I was thinking of grabbing some treats. Let me fix it real quick." In reality, she just wanted a few moments alone.

For so long, he had acted like an irresponsible teenager, she thought as she headed to the kitchen. He did nothing unless prompted – even simple tasks like cleaning the house or getting groceries required her to ask for help. They had stopped having their weekly date nights, with him often claiming to be too tired or sleepy. She made up her mind to talk to him about it as soon as they were alone.

When she entered the room with an assortment of chips, chocolates, and nuts, everyone cheered for her. Throughout the night, she continued to wear a smile, despite feeling hurt.
