Have you ever heard a rumor about yourself?

Written By NT June 21, 2023
Toxicity

She was as high as the sky. They were sitting on their balcony with her boyfriend, just listening to some chill music. She just wanted to feel nothing that day, not feeling the weight of that rumor at her work, doesn't want to feel the humiliation that she had never experienced in her life, doesn't want to feel the disappointment that she experienced today.

She felt abused, she felt hurted so much, she could even feel the pain in her body. Her brain shut down itself after she heard the saying.She slept through the whole day. But she was awake again. She was a good employer she knew, she was getting the job done before even her senior. Just a couple of months ago, they moved in together with her boyfriend. It was a 1 bedroom, with a little balcony. They build this life together. She doesn't even remember when was the last time she felt that happy.

Today though, she remembered the things that she buried a long time ago. But now she remembers that feeling, that pain in her body. She knew that feeling, that abused feeling, it was so funny how our brain works she thought. She suddenly remembered the incident, it was that summer, when she felt the public humiliation for the first time in her life. When she was screaming to her father to stop, and no one was coming to help them. Once she stepped outside everyone was there, just listening to the scream of the 13 year old girl and the mother. And again, she felt the same humiliation, desperation. Only difference this time there was someone with her, she wasn't screaming but there he was, she wasn't alone anymore, there was someone to hold her hand, telling her that everything is going to be okay.
