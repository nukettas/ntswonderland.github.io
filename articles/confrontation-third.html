Have you ever had one of those f*cked up days?

Written By NT June 17, 2023
Confrontation

She woke up feeling grumpy today. Not only was she unwell, but she also felt an underlying sense of anger. Hoping to lift her spirits, she took a sip of her morning coffee and decided to call her mom. Her mother always had a way of energizing her. After two unsuccessful attempts, she gave up, realizing her mom was likely busy. This only added to her growing grumpiness.

A few hours before she had to leave for work, she planned to study, have breakfast. However, as she tried to focus on her studies, concentration eluded her. She had a really difficult time focusing when she first started studying. She attempted to study for 30 minutes, but all she could comprehend was her mounting rage. She was biting her lip, her knee was trembling, and her hands were sweating. Frustrated, she abandoned her study plans and opted to watch Netflix.

Even while indulging in her favorite show, her leg continued to shake. Nevertheless, she brushed it off, thinking, "Whatever."

As she walked to work, she felt invisible. People were just fucking walking towards her as if she wasn't even there! "Am I really that unnoticeable? That unimportant?" she wondered. Just as she was about to cross the street, a car failed to stop for her, while driving right by. On the subway, she witnessed a fight right beside her. "What the heck is wrong with everyone today?" she asked herself. Finally, arriving at work, she put on her apron and started to talk about how horrible her day was with her work bestie. While she was talking, realized it was just one of those days.
