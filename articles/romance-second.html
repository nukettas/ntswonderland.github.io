Have you ever fall in love?

Written By NT June 7, 2023
Romance

"She likes to be by herself occasionally," he told his friend. “She enjoys being alone and has a social battery. I simply enjoy seeing her as she moves around while smiling a little”. He laughed and said, "She's definitely lost in her own world”. He began to play with the sand while looking down.“You are freaking in love, Brat. Just ask her to move in with you, would you?” said George,” Brat chuckled nervously, "I'm not in love, I can't be," he told himself.

Surely that should only happen once in a lifetime. I can't fall in love again; the last time I did, I was miserable for a whole year. As he waved his hands to Brat, George said, "And Brat has left the building." Brat chuckled, saying, "Let's go eat something, I'm starving. Lisa, Lisa! Please, let's leave.” As though awakening from a dream, she leaped. Brat was utterly bewildered and suddenly craved solitude. He wanted to go and eat something right away. He casually put his arm over Lisa's neck, kissed her, and then let her go when she asked to hold his hand while they were walking. All of a sudden, he felt detached, and he really wanted some time alone, cannot let anyone make him that pathetic ever again..
