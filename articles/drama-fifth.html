Have you ever been cheated on?

Written By NT June 30, 2023
Drama

As soon as she woke up and saw his boyfriend lying next to her, she felt the sadness as she felt every night for 3 months. They become more and more distant over time. They were having fights as soon as they started to talk. “Before I get into those depressing thoughts I should just start studying” she thought and got up, made her morning coffee, turned on her computer and checked her emails. “Oh he must have forgotten to close his account on my computer”, since his phone has broken, he was using her computer.

She saw that the instagram was still open. She felt this urge to check his DM’s. She fought for a couple of minutes but she couldn't help herself and started to dig in. She saw that message sent by his “best friend”. “What are we going to do now? How are we going to talk?” She read it 3 times, and couldn't make any sense. “Why was this woman so anxious about talking with my boyfriend?” she asked. And why the hell on earth the only deleted messages were from that woman? There were no previous messages, only those few sentences.

She felt the anger and anxiety on her head. Her eyes started twitching. Now she was thinking about digging into his whatsapp also. She was about to ruin every single stand in their relationship, the personal space, boundaries. She wasn't thinking clearly of course, and she tried to use the whatsapp web and there it was all the messages. But again only a few sentences from her, other texts were there. “Why was he deleting the conversations?” She rushed into the bed, woke him up and it was what she hoped not to. He was cheating for two fucking months with his best friend. She packed her bag, took a few things and left the house that once she felt secure, safe, peaceful.
