Reawakened Ties

Written By NT July 16, 2023
+18

"Hey, Mel." "Hi, Dimitri." "I heard you've moved back to town." "Yeah, I did. It's good to see you." "Likewise." Nine days had passed since Dimitri and I exchanged pleasantries, but something about him gave me an uneasy feeling. "Hey, would you like to grab a coffee? I wanted to talk about something," he suggested, his demeanor slightly off.

We sat down for our coffee, but Dimitri seemed preoccupied. Then he dropped a bombshell. "What do you think about Emmayarmes?" Confused, I assumed he was referring to her content since we were both content creators. "Yeah, she's really cool. I enjoy her work. Why do you ask?" Dimitri's tone took an unexpected turn. "Well, the other day we were discussing you and your content, and she told me she's been having dreams about you while cooking, even during intimate moments. We just wanted to check if you're into certain things."

My confusion deepened. "Certain things? Like what?" He hesitated before revealing intimate details. "I have some issues in bed. I only masturbate, and I'm not really interested in sex anymore. I've seen your Instagram dance videos, and you're just amazing. I don't mean anything negative by it, but if you're open to it, we were thinking of watching you two." Taken aback, I asked, "She's already agreed to this?" Dimitri nodded. "Yeah, we discussed it." Suddenly, Emmayarmes entered the room, introducing herself before leaning in to kiss me. We found ourselves dancing, our connection undeniable. The following morning, we awoke together, shared breakfast, and went shopping as if the previous day's events hadn't transpired.
