Unforeseen Interview

Written By NT August 14, 2023
+18

"Hey, I stumbled upon the perfect job, but it's a group thing. Would you be interested in joining me?" Lily asked with excitement. Curiosity piqued, Mark inquired, "What kind of job are we talking about here?" Lily's grin widened. "I discovered it online. I don't have the full details, but they've scheduled an interview for 6 pm. They mentioned that if I could, I should bring friends along. Are you both up for it?"

Laura chimed in, "Sure, why not? Let's see what this is all about." The three of us made our way to the specified location. Upon reaching the entrance, we were met by two girls dressed in angel costumes who welcomed us warmly and led us inside. As the interview progressed, it became clear that this wasn't your typical job opportunity.

The interviewer began describing the job requirements, and a strange realization dawned upon us. We were supposed to simply sit and create the illusion of a bustling environment, encouraging others to believe the place was packed. And if anyone wanted to engage in conversation, we were encouraged to oblige. It was then that the truth hit us like a ton of bricks – Lily had unknowingly found us a job at a strip club!

Shocked and slightly bewildered, we exchanged uncertain glances. This was definitely not what any of us had expected.
