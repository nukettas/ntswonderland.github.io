Have you ever been assaulted by your best friend?

Written By NT June 26, 2023
Friendship

It was an idyllic day for their long-awaited reunion. The clock ticked towards 3 pm, marking the start of her work shift, while the heavens smiled down with a radiant sun after an extended spell of rain. The embracing warmth of sunny days had finally returned. Sam's call inviting her for a leisurely stroll and an Irish coffee was too enticing to resist. The prospect of reconnecting after months filled her with eager anticipation.

As she readied herself, thoughts of the three-month gap since their last meeting crossed her mind, which she shared with her boyfriend while preparing. Adorning her work attire, she added delicate jewelry that caught the light just right, dabbed her favored perfume, and applied a touch of makeup to enhance her features. Fully prepared, she left her abode, a vision of poise and confidence.

Their rendezvous point was set for 11 am, at the bustling intersection of Bloor and Bay. Punctuality was her hallmark, and she arrived precisely at 10:50. However, as the hands of the clock marched on to 11:35, she couldn't help but entertain thoughts of the inconsiderateness of keeping someone waiting. At long last, Sam emerged, expressing his apologies and explaining that work had held him hostage in conversations and engagements. Suppressing any frustration, she graced him with a smile, and they mutually agreed to embark on their coffee outing.

Their exchange initially revolved around their professional lives, but soon took an unexpected turn. Sam's inquiry into her earnings and the challenges of sustaining life in Toronto caught her off guard. The topic was an unwelcome departure from her anticipated conversation. A sense of unease began to pervade her. "Becoming a bartender at a local pub might yield more than your current role," Sam suggested, recounting anecdotes of a friend who had held a similar job. He proceeded to describe her, his eyes lingering in a way that made Kira feel uneasy. The conversation veered into displaying photos and tactless body shaming remarks about Kira.

As the coffee shop interaction drew to a close, Kira felt emotionally wounded and vulnerable. Stepping outside, she grappled with a surge of self-doubt. The sharp words Sam had casually uttered had taken root, prompting her to question her own perception of her beauty and worth.
