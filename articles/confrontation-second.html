What is your last accomplishment?

Written By NT June 15, 2023
Confrontation

While cutting her own hair in the bathroom, she found herself engrossed in a series of introspective questions. Thoughts like "When did she arrive in Canada? How long had it been since she last saw her best friend get drunk with her? Has she changed much since they last saw her?" flooded her mind with each snip of the scissors. She was trimming the back of her hair when she abruptly stopped because she had noticed those worn yet hopeful eyes, which lacked sparkle. Despite her dissatisfaction with her reflection, she proved she can handle everything on her own. In the mirror, she sees tiredness and pride. Although she felt helpless, she was actually happy. She had even managed to cut her own hair—an accomplishment worth celebrating, considering it was always a hairdresser cutting her hair, a nail artist who does her nails, a driver who picks her up and drops her off.It was undeniably a proud moment, even though she had made a mess of the back of her hair.

Now, she stood in front of the mirror, observing herself with a broad smile, despite the absurdity of her hair. It dawned on her at that very moment that she had never truly been alone. She realized that she had always had a boyfriend, never letting a month pass without some form of romantic entanglement. A disconcerting thought immediately nagged at her: "Had she been molding her personality to fit the desires of each man who entered her life? Was she just a copycat? Then who was she truly? What was her authentic style?" She had never experienced loneliness, as someone always came and went. Despite the fact that she had found comfort in this situation, she questioned her genuine happiness. Was she really satisfied or simply letting things go? By allowing any man who expressed interest to become a part of her life, was she genuinely happy?

As she cleaned the sink, the scissors, and the floor, she hoped for the best while preparing herself for the worst.
