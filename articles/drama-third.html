Have you ever been fooled?

Written By NT June 23, 2023
Drama

"He's ticking all the right boxes," she exclaimed, her smile lighting up the room. Sarah felt a wave of relief wash over her as her friend delivered those words. They were deep in conversation about a man she had recently crossed paths with, a mere seven weeks ago. After a series of disappointing dates, this new person had brought genuine happiness into her life.

Unlike the others, he had taken the initiative to ask her out on a proper date. With thoughtfulness, he had selected the venue, a local pub where he expressed his desire to simply be in her company. "Just being around you," he had admitted that night, his smile offering a reassuring comfort.
He had disclosed his upcoming three-week trip with her buddy, ensuring that their second date would align just before his departure. Even while away, he maintained regular contact, sending her messages three or four times a week. These messages held a reassuring quality that eased Sarah's underlying fears.

Yet, amidst the green flags waving before her, Sarah couldn't shake a growing sense of unease. Deep down, she acknowledged that perfection was elusive in life. Even his promise to cook for her upon his return seemed too good to be true. Her trepidation remained undefined, though its weight was undeniable. The day's cheesecake was shared with Kira, concluding their time together.

Two days later, they found themselves cooking side by side, accompanied by wine, laughter, and a touch of tipsiness. Amidst the playful atmosphere, their connection grew, and the night ended with tender kisses scattered across cheeks, chin, nose, and forehead. Sarah's heart raced, her emotions dancing to a fervent rhythm. As the days ticked by, nine sunsets later, a message arrived, commencing with an apology. The weight of uncertainty settled upon her chest as she read those words. Swamped with work, she couldn't dedicate her full attention to the message, but a quick glance revealed its essence. She replied with a gentle "no worries :)," her heart heavy with a growing sensation she couldn't grasp. The message conveyed gratitude for shared moments, yet offered no explanation or reason. The whys remained elusive, and Sarah felt drained, as though she had been awake for two days straight. Nonetheless, she soldiered on, burying herself in work despite the fatigue that clung to her.
