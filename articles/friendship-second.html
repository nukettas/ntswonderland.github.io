Have you ever got stabbed in the back?

Written By NT July 10, 2023
Friendship

"He's been asking about you. What's the conversation about?" Kira asked Sarah with an air of excitement as she skillfully concocted drinks for the customers. "Just casual chatter. What did he want to know?" Sarah responded while continuing her work. "He was curious if you're in a relationship or if there's something serious going on," Kira revealed with a certain eagerness. "He's quite the handsome guy, and a dentist to boot. Let's see where this leads. Oh my!" she exclaimed.

The night flowed on, and the trio engaged in lengthy conversations with the dentist. His curiosity delved into Kira's personal life, prompting him to seek comprehensive knowledge. After four hours, exhaustion began to creep in on Sarah, compelling her to bid adieu. "I can give you a lift," he offered. "It's not far, really, you don't have to trouble yourself. You're still seated," Sarah politely countered. Yet, his persistence prevailed, and he insisted on accompanying her. "Just give me a moment! Allow me to join you; I could use a breather for about ten minutes," he requested. "What about the pub? Who will manage it?" Sarah inquired. "Ethan can handle it. It's not overly bustling right now." With that settled, they embarked on their departure.

A fleeting melancholy expression crossed the dentist's face as he looked at Sarah, and soon enough, laughter enveloped them both. In an attempt to connect, he showcased his playlist, fostering a discussion about their musical preferences. However, Kira's chatter seemed to have no off switch. When the time came to part ways, his embrace was unexpectedly tight. As she left, the memory of that lingering hug remained with her.

The next day brought a surprising text from Kira. "He's a great kisser," it read. Flabbergasted, Sarah responded, "Who on earth did you make out with? Spill the beans!" Kira's response was equally shocking, "The dentist. We kissed passionately for a good half-hour." Confusion filled Sarah's mind. "Wait, what? I thought it was just a casual thing, a whim. We talked, and when we kissed, he didn't pull away. That's all. Are you upset?" Kira retorted. "No, I'm actually glad you've found someone. Enjoy!" Sarah forced out a supportive response, concealing her feelings of betrayal.

Deeply hurt, Sarah grappled with the sensation of betrayal, yet she found herself unable to voice her emotions.
