Have you ever cried on your birthday?

Written By NT June 27, 2023
Toxicity

"And here we go again," she said, realizing that in three days she would turn 26. She already knew the plan - she would be waiting at home for her friends. When they arrived with balloons and cake, she would pretend to be surprised, spend the evening together, and wash the dishes afterward. She appreciated this small, thoughtful gesture. Despite telling everyone that she didn't like celebrating her birthday, that was actually a lie.

She still remembers the disappointment she felt two years ago when she expected a grand celebration from her boyfriend. They had talked about getting married, and she anticipated grand gestures from him. Meanwhile, she had put in a lot of effort to make him happy. But all she got was a kiss. "I didn't buy a cake because you don't like it, but if you want, we can go and eat some cheesecake," he said. She tried to hide her sadness and pretended that everything was fine since it was a football night after all. She let it pass that year.

The following year, she got so drunk on her birthday that she decided to call her brother and share the song that was playing, as it reminded her of their childhood and was his favorite song. After three minutes of yelling and asking why she called since they talked in the morning, he abruptly hung up. She was confused because she couldn't even understand the reason for his anger, let alone ask about it. That year, she cried and made the decision to stop celebrating her birthday altogether.
