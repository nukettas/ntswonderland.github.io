Have you ever thought that your family might not love you as you think?

Written By NT July 7, 2023
Confrontation

When they were talking with her boyfriend about how they would raise a baby together, she couldn't help Herself but wondered how she would be if some lovely non alcoholic couple would raise her. Would she be getting that cello class she always wanted when she was 13. She remembered her fathers response when she asked if she could try that. He said that his dream was getting a helicopter so not everybody's dream would come true. Then she remembered, wasn't he the one who took piano lessons a couple months after she asked for that class.

When she was drawing clothes just for herself, butterfly dress, and pink cute pants with flower, when she told her mom that she wanted to become a designer and wanted to live in Spain doesn't she the one who told her no designer make money unless she is a very talented, and with a smug smile on her face throw the paper of her designs when she was only 11.

When she hit 17 and loved to be a Baker and open up her coffee shop, wasn't her mom told her if she became a Baker she would end up broke. Then she remembered the time when she learned how to write when she was in kindergarten and they hung her shirt story on the wall about how proud she was. And a couple months later when her father finds one of her stories and ripped it off while he was drunk. What could she become if someone else, even with slightly different parents, raised her. Maybe she has more self esteem who knows maybe she would even own a coffee shop that she always wanted.
